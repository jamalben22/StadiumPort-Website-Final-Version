INTERNAL LINKING STRATEGY MATRIX
================================

1. HOMEPAGE
   - Primary Links: Host Cities Hub, Stadiums Hub, Prediction Game, Groups & Schedule.
   - Anchor Text: "Explore Host Cities", "View Stadium Guides", "Play Prediction Game".

2. HOST CITY PAGES (e.g., /world-cup-2026-host-cities/atlanta)
   - Link TO:
     - Associated Stadium (Mercedes-Benz Stadium)
     - Accommodation Guide for that city
     - Transport Guide for that city
     - Safety Guide
   - Link FROM:
     - Homepage
     - Host Cities Hub
     - Relevant Articles (e.g., "Best BBQ in Atlanta")

3. STADIUM PAGES (e.g., /world-cup-2026-stadiums/mercedes-benz-stadium)
   - Link TO:
     - Host City Page
     - Ticket Information
     - Seating Chart Article
     - Parking/Transport Article
   - Link FROM:
     - Homepage
     - Stadiums Hub
     - Host City Page
     - Schedule Pages (matches played there)

4. GROUP PAGES (e.g., /groups/group-a)
   - Link TO:
     - Stadiums hosting Group A matches
     - Schedule Page
     - Prediction Game
   - Link FROM:
     - Groups Hub
     - Homepage
     - Participating Team Pages

5. ARTICLES / GUIDES
   - Structure: Hub & Spoke (Topic Clusters)
   - Pillar Page: "Travel Guides" linking to specific guides.
   - Specific Guide: Link back to Pillar, link to related City/Stadium.
   - In-Content: 3-5 internal links per 1000 words.

ANCHOR TEXT BEST PRACTICES:
- Avoid "Click here".
- Use descriptive, keyword-rich phrases (e.g., "Find hotels near AT&T Stadium", "View full Group A schedule").
- Vary anchor text to look natural.
